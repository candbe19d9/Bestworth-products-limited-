About Us

BestWorth Products Limited is a proudly Nigerian manufacturing company


committed to producing top-quality building materials for lasting excellence. Incorporated in September 2017 and commencing operations in December 2024 at our factory in Epe, Lagos, we are driven by a mission to support the construction industry with durable, efficient, and affordable products. Our operations are guided by a business model that ensures all stakeholders customers, employees, partners, and communities benefit from our continued growth. BestWorth Products Limited is steadily building a strong and trusted brand in the building materials sector.


Our Vision
To be a leading manufacturer of reliable and affordable building materials in Nigeria, known for innovation, quality, and stakeholder value

Our Mission
To manufacture high-quality building materials that meet the needs of our customers, delivered through efficient operations and a commitment to excellence.

Core Values

Quality: We ensure every product meets high durability and performance standards.

Efficiency: We optimize our processes to deliver products on time and at scale.

Affordability: We provide value without compromising on quality.

Customer Focus: We support our clients with flexible minimum order quantities and reliable service.

Integrity: We are honest and transparent in all business dealings.

Shared Success: We are committed to delivering value to all stakeholders.

Our Products

At Bestworth Products Limited, we specialize in manufacturing high-quality steel nails ranging from 1 to 5 inches. And many more essential construction materials. With flexible minimum orders for users and large distributors, ensuring accessibility and convenience for all customers. Our expanding product lines are: Roofing nails, drywall screws, binding wire, and wire meshes.

Retail Services

Best Worth Builder's Mart is a trusted retail business based in Lagos, offering both a physical store and online platform to meet the building material needs of construction and home improvement projects.


We are dedicated to being a one-stop store for all building materials, providing reliable and affordable products alongside excellent customer service.
